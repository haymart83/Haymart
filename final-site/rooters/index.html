How To Use This Tool

Getting Started

1
Choose Your Facebook Page(s)
At the top, tick which pages to post to. The Rooters (Main) is the primary profile. Rooters Classic Rock is the secondary page. Tick both to post to both. The website (www.therooters.co.uk) is auto-added to every post - untick to remove it.
2
Navigate
Top nav (PLAN) - view and manage your calendar. Bottom nav (CREATE) - write new posts. Start on MONTH to see your full schedule.

Planning Views

📅
Month View
See the whole month. Amber markers = actual gig dates (auto-loaded from the website). Tap any date to add a post. AUTO THIS MONTH instantly generates all 4 promo posts for every gig that month.
📆
Week View
Hour-by-hour view. Tap any slot to schedule a post at that exact time. Scroll sideways to see all 7 days.
📋
Queue (List View)
All posts in date order. Filter by Draft, Ready, or Approved. Tap OK to approve a post. APPROVE ALL READY bulk-approves everything. Tap EDIT to change caption or reschedule.
💡
Content Bank
Generated posts without a date. Tap + SCHEDULE on any bank item to pick a date and move it to the queue.

Creating Posts

✎
General Post
Any type of post. Pick type, enter topic, choose tone. GENERATE AND QUEUE sends it to the queue. TO BANK saves it without a date yet.
🎸
Gig Promos
Pick any gig from the scrollable list - all 2026 dates pre-loaded. Claude generates 4 posts per gig: one week out, 3 days out, TONIGHT!, and post-gig thanks.
⚡
Auto-Generate
Select months and hit AUTO-GENERATE. Claude writes all 4 promo posts for every gig automatically. Everything goes in as Drafts - review and approve in Queue.
Ned's best workflow: Auto-generate a month at a time, go to Queue, read through each post, mark as Approved. Takes about 10 minutes to set up weeks of content.

Post Status

D
Draft
Just generated - needs a read-through. Auto-generated posts always start as Drafts.
R
Ready
Reviewed and happy with it. Manual posts go straight to Ready.
A
Approved
Fully signed off and confirmed for posting.
IMPORTANT - Posting to Facebook:

This tool generates and schedules content - it does NOT post to Facebook automatically. To post: go to Queue, find today's post, copy the caption, paste into Facebook with your photo. The website link is already in every caption.

For true auto-posting in future, ask Thomas about connecting to the Facebook Graph API or Buffer.

✅Legitimate — Safe & Effective
💬
Manual DMs to new followers
Perfectly fine on every platform. The templates are starting points — tweak a detail for each person. Platforms allow this because it is genuine human behaviour.
💭
Commenting on other posts
The highest-ROI engagement activity available. Thoughtful comments in your niche get you seen by the account owner AND their audience. 20–30 per day manually is safe and effective.
❤️
Liking posts manually
Completely fine. Stay under ~150 likes per day to avoid temporary action blocks. The strategy tool keeps you well within this.
↩️
Replying to your own comments
Not just allowed — platforms actively reward it. Replying within the first hour of posting significantly boosts algorithmic reach. Always reply to everyone.
👥
Following accounts in your niche
Fine manually. Stay under 100–150 new follows per day. Do not immediately unfollow — follow/unfollow churning is detectable and against the rules.
📅
Third-party post schedulers (Buffer, Later, Hootsuite)
Officially sanctioned by Meta and TikTok via their APIs. Scheduling posts is fine. Scheduling engagement actions (likes, comments) is not.
🤖
ManyChat / chatbot DMs triggered by the user
Meta-approved when the user initiates the interaction — e.g. "Comment TICKETS to get the link." Unprompted automated DMs are not allowed.
❌Not Allowed — Can Get You Banned
🤖
Automated liking, commenting, following or DMing
Violates Terms of Service on every platform. Meta, TikTok and YouTube have dedicated ML systems to detect non-human behaviour patterns. Penalties range from temporary blocks to permanent removal.
💸
Buying followers or engagement
Against the rules everywhere, and counterproductive. Fake followers do not engage — this tanks your engagement rate and tells the algorithm your content is low quality. Platforms purge fake accounts regularly.
📨
Mass identical DMs sent manually
Even sent by hand, sending the exact same message to hundreds of people in a short window gets flagged as spam. The tool generates 5 variants so you always rotate.
🔄
Comment pods and coordinated engagement groups
Groups where members agree to like and comment on demand. Instagram has been actively suppressing these since 2022 and the behaviour is explicitly prohibited.
🔁
Posting identical comments repeatedly
If the same comment text appears across multiple posts in a session, it is treated as spam. Always use different variants from the tool.
⚠️Grey Zone — Proceed With Caution
🔄
Follow / unfollow strategies
Technically allowed if done slowly and manually, but bad practice. People notice and it creates a poor impression. Not recommended.
📊
Engagement rate gaming
Artificially inflating metrics through any coordinated means undermines long-term growth and can trigger algorithmic suppression even if no rules are explicitly broken.
THE BOTTOM LINE
The engagement hub is built around the one approach that always works and never gets accounts banned: showing up as a real person, consistently, every day. 20–30 minutes of genuine manual engagement per day — leaving real comments, replying to everyone, sending personal DMs to new followers — will outperform any bot strategy within 60–90 days, and the results compound over time.
Algorithms on every platform are specifically designed to reward human behaviour. The tool removes the friction of thinking what to write — the sending is always you.
